VISWIN Large Art Storage Rack

A versatile and durable solution for artists, designers, and creative professionals. Perfect for storing, displaying, or drying your artwork with confidence and style.

Key Features

  • Large capacity for canvases, prints, and artboards
  • Stable wooden frame with wide base for added safety
  • Lockable casters for easy mobility and secure positioning
  • Non-slip platform that prevents artwork from shifting
  • Easy assembly with clear instructions and labeled hardware

Why Choose This Rack?

This art storage rack is designed to keep your creative projects organized and protected. Its spacious non-slip platform ensures that your canvases, posters, and artboards stay in place whether you're working or displaying.

Constructed from sturdy pine wood, it offers both durability and a natural aesthetic that complements any creative space. The reinforced base provides stability even when fully loaded, while lockable casters make it easy to move from one area to another.

Whether you're in a home studio, classroom, or professional gallery, this rack is an elegant and practical addition to your workspace. It's also ideal as a gift for any art enthusiast who values both organization and care for their artwork.
